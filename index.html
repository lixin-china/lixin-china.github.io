About me

I am now a Ph.D. candidate at ShanghaiTech University. I am also with the Shanghai Institute of Microsystem and Information Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research advisor is Professor Junrui Liang. My research interests include kinetic energy harvesting, intermittent computing, ubiquitous computing, and battery-free IoT.
